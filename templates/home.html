Satellite Image Classification

Using the DUIx xView Dataset

The governement agency Defense Unit Innovation Experimental (DUIx) has provided one of the largest pre-classified publicly available datasets of overhead imagery.

Octo Consulting Group has worked with Virginia Tech Computational Modeling and Data Analytics students to improve upon a pre-existing Convolutional Neural Network model. The algorithm can accurately detect and classify roughly 60 objects that are seen in most satellite images.

Now that this technology exists, things such as distaster relief and troop supply movement can be improved.
